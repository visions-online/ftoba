## Why This Site Exists

This repository was created to provide public access to court documents that some parties have attempted to keep hidden. We believe that transparency in the legal process is essential for justice. Here, you'll find documents related to lawsuits against the Oklahoma Bar Association, Oklahoma Supreme Court, and other implicated entities.

## What You'll Find

- **Full Federal Lawsuit Filings:** Review the 300+ page complaint and related filings.
- **Exhibits and Evidence:** Access over 1,000 pages of supporting exhibits submitted to federal court.
- **Emergency Motions:** Read the emergency injunctive relief applications and related pleadings.
- **Educational Templates:** Use our legal templates as a starting point for your own filings.
