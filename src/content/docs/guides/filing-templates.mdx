## Legal Document Templates

While our primary mission is to expose corruption through transparency, we maintain these legal document templates to help individuals effectively engage with the legal system. These templates can be particularly useful when filing documents related to systemic issues in Oklahoma's courts.

## Available Templates

### Federal Court Templates
- **Complaints:** Initial filing templates
- **Motions:** Various motion formats
- **Responses:** Reply document templates
- **Declarations:** Sworn statement formats
- **Exhibits:** Exhibit list templates

### State Court Templates
- **Petitions:** Initial filing formats
- **Applications:** Various request types
- **Responses:** Answer templates
- **Motions:** Standard motion formats
- **Orders:** Proposed order templates

- Verify court requirements

### Document Customization
